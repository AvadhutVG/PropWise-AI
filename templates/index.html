How Price is Estimated

PropWise-AI estimates property prices using a Ridge Regression model trained on cleaned Bangalore housing data. The prediction is based on five key inputs:

  • Location – One-hot encoded to capture locality-specific trends.
  • Total Area (sq.ft.) – Larger homes generally command higher prices.
  • BHK – Represents the number of bedrooms.
  • Bathrooms – Additional bathrooms often increase property value.
  • Balconies – Included as an additional lifestyle feature.

Before deployment, multiple regression algorithms were evaluated. Ridge Regression achieved the highest R² score (0.8114) and was selected as the final production model.
